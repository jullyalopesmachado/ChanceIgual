Our mission

Providing free language classes, Chance Igual wishes to help build a future generation of adults who are passionate for learning and exporing other languages and cultures. Founded in 2021, Chance Igual is an organization dedicated to promoting free, accessible education for children and teenagers in underrepresented regions across Brazil. Its mission is to bridge educational gaps by offering online classes, ensuring that even those in remote or underserved communities have the opportunity to learn and grow. Focusing primarily on language education, Chance Igual offers a range of free language courses designed to ignite a passion for learning among young people. The organization believes that mastering new languages not only opens doors to diverse cultures but also helps foster global awareness and a broader worldview. By empowering the next generation with linguistic skills and cultural understanding, Chance Igual aims to cultivate a future generation of well-rounded adults who are both eager to explore the world and capable of contributing meaningfully to society.
